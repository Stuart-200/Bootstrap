Football

Football is a popular outdoor game played between two teams of eleven players each.

The main rule is that players cannot use their hands or arms to touch the ball, except for the goalkeeper. The aim is to score goals by kicking the ball into the opponents net. A football match lasts 90 minutes and is controlled by a referee. Football is loved all over the world and has many famous players like Lionel Messi, Cristiano Ronaldo, and Neymar Jr. One of the biggest tournaments in football is the

World Cup which takes place every four years

and features teams from around the world competing for the championship. Football brings people together,so
